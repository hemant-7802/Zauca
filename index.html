    GST & Establishment Registration

    Online Documents submission and hassle-free GST Registration in 3 days

    Why GST Registration? You can get yourself registered under GST if your annual turnover is more than Rs. 40
    lakhs. Also, if you are doing online business (selling through amazon, flipkart etc.), you are required to get a GST
    number. For registering under GST, you need the following documents

    Sole Proprietor – Rs. 1500

    1. Trade Name
    2. Nature of Service
    3. Pan Card
    4. Aadar Card
    5. Passport Size Photo
    6. Proof of business place (Rental Agreement/ Electricity Bill/ Municipal Tax Paid Receipt)
    7. Cancelled Cheque (Savings or Current Account)


    Shops and Establishments Registration – Rs.1999

    The Shops and Establishments Act regulates conditions of work, lists rights of employees in the unorganised sector and provides a list of obligations for every employer. It applies nationwide to shops, commercial establishments, hotels, restaurants, eating houses, theatres and other places of public amusement or entertainments. Every shop and establishment is required to register itself under the Act within 30 days of commencement of work, whether or not it has employees.
